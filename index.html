Overview

PetCare+ is a smart, all-in-one platform designed to simplify pet care for owners. Unlike traditional apps that only offer reminders, PetCare+ brings together health tracking, allergy management, diet and supplement planning, appointments, breed-specific guidance, and AI chat support in one place. With clear reports and analytics, pet owners can easily monitor their pet’s well-being and make informed decisions anytime, from desktop or mobile.
